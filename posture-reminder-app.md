# Posture Reminder Desktop App

## Problem
Many people working at a computer forget to maintain healthy posture and take breaks.

## Goal
Design a simple desktop tool that reminds users to adjust posture without interrupting their workflow.

## UX Focus
- Minimal interface
- Non-intrusive reminders
- Clear feedback

## Technology
Python desktop application.

## Outcome
A lightweight reminder tool designed to support healthier computer habits through subtle interaction.
